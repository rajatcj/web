About Parbat Dental

Where Modern Dentistry Meets Genuine Care

Parbat Dental was founded with a single mission: to make high-quality dental care accessible, affordable, and stress-free for every family in Pokhara. Located at the heart of Prithvi Chowk, we've earned the trust of thousands of patients across the valley.

Our clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by a team of passionate professionals who genuinely care about your smile and your overall health.

Specialist-Led Care Led by Dr. Shristi Sapkota (BDS, MDS) — Consultant Orthodontist, NMC No. 11307
Prime Location Conveniently located at Prithvi Chowk — the center of Pokhara city, easy to access from all areas.
Patient-First Culture We treat every patient like family. Your comfort, trust, and satisfaction drive everything we do.
Modern Equipment Digital X-rays, rotary endodontics, and advanced orthodontic tools for precise, lasting results.
