Our Story

From a small practice to a trusted name in Nepalese dentistry — this is our journey.

Dynamic Dental Clinic was founded with a single, powerful mission: to bring world-class dental care to the people of Nepal at affordable prices. What started as a passionate dream has grown into a multi-specialist practice serving patients across Pokhara and Ghorahi.

Led by Dr. Rashmita Basnet, our clinic has grown to include a team of NMC-certified specialists, state-of-the-art equipment, and a culture of compassionate care. We believe that everyone deserves a healthy, beautiful smile — and we work every day to make that a reality.

With over 1,000 happy patients and a 4.6-star rating on Google, our commitment to excellence speaks for itself. We're not just dentists — we're partners in your lifelong oral health journey.
