New Model Secondary School

Learn · Innovate · Accelerate

One of the most reputed schools of Pokhar, a well-established, privately run co-educational institution with a long, glorious history. Situated in the surroundings of Nagdhunga, Pokhara, we have nurtured generations of bright, capable citizens.

Who We Are

Built on Values, Driven by Vision

Our school combines academic rigour with a warm, inclusive environment that helps every student discover their potential.

🎯 Mission

To develop, strengthen and expand educational schemes and facilities for quality education in order to meet the changing needs of today's children.

🔭 Vision

To create an educational world in which children of all backgrounds and from all corners of the country have an opportunity for quality and affordable education.

🤝 Commitment

To provide modern education at the forefront of professional practice in Nepal, helping children realize their full potential and develop positive attitudes towards life.
