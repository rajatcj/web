How to Apply

Admission Process

1

Submit Enquiry

Fill out the enquiry form on this page or visit the school office in person to express your interest and get detailed information about the admission process.

2

Collect Application Form

Obtain the official admission application form from the school office. The form is available during school working hours (10 AM – 4 PM, Sunday to Friday).

3

Submit Documents

Submit the completed application form along with all required documents (see below) to the school office before the application deadline.

4

Entrance Assessment

Eligible applicants may be called for a brief entrance assessment and/or parent-student interview with the school administration.

5

Admission Confirmation

Selected students will be notified of their admission status. Upon confirmation, the admission fee must be paid within the specified time to secure the seat.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

When can I apply for admission?

+
Admissions are open from Baisakh (April/May) each year for the upcoming academic session. We recommend applying early as seats are limited. Visit the school or enquire online to check the exact dates.

Is there an entrance test for admission?

+
For some grade levels, a brief entrance assessment may be conducted. This helps the school understand the student's academic level and ensure appropriate grade placement. Details are communicated after the application is submitted.

Are scholarships available?

+
Yes, New Model offers scholarships to meritorious and financially deserving students. Criteria and application procedure are available from the school office. Students are encouraged to inquire about scholarship eligibility during the admission process.

What co-curricular activities does the school offer?

+
We offer a wide range of co-curricular activities including sports, debates, cultural programs, student council, and various club activities. We believe in holistic development beyond academics.

How will I be notified about the admission status?

+
Admission results are communicated through phone calls to the contact number provided in the application form. You can also visit the school office or check the notice board for results.

Is transport facility available from the first day?

+
Yes, transport facility is available from the first day of school. Students must register for the transport service separately and pay the applicable monthly transport fee. Routes cover all major areas of Pokhara valley.

What is the medium of instruction?

+
New Model follows the national curriculum with instruction primarily in Nepali, with strong emphasis on English language learning. English is used as the medium for Computer Science and certain other subjects.
