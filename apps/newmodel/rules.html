Rules & Regulations

Our school guidelines are designed to create a safe, respectful, and productive environment where every student can thrive.

These rules and regulations apply to all students enrolled at New Model Secondary School. Students and parents/guardians are required to read, understand, and comply with these guidelines. Violation of these rules may result in disciplinary action.

📋

General Conduct

Expected behavior and values in school

  • 1
    All students must greet teachers and staff respectfully and maintain courteous behavior within and outside school premises.
  • 2
    Students must respect school property. Any damage to school property will be treated seriously and the cost of repair will be borne by the responsible student's family.
  • 3
    Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited during school hours. Phones must be switched off and kept in bags at all times on campus.
  • 4
    Students are prohibited from bringing any harmful objects, tobacco, alcohol, or illegal substances onto school premises.
  • 5
    Students must maintain cleanliness and hygiene — disposing of waste properly and keeping their classrooms, corridors and campus clean.
  • 6
    Bullying, harassment or discrimination of any kind is strictly forbidden and will result in immediate disciplinary action.
  • 7
    Students must speak respectfully to peers and refrain from using abusive language, gestures, or behavior.
🕐

Attendance & Punctuality

Time and presence requirements

  • 1
    Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance to be eligible to sit for the annual examination.
  • 2
    Punctuality is mandatory. Students must arrive before the school bell rings. Late arrivals without valid reason will be marked and reported to parents.
  • 3
    In case of absence, a written leave application signed by the parent/guardian must be submitted on the day of return.
  • 4
    For medical absences exceeding 3 days, a doctor's medical certificate must be provided along with the leave application.
  • 5
    Students leaving school premises during school hours must obtain prior permission from the class teacher or school office.
👔

Uniform & Appearance

Dress code and personal presentation

  • 1
    All students must wear the prescribed school uniform in a neat and tidy condition every school day.
  • 2
    The school uniform must be properly pressed and clean. Torn or dirty uniforms are not acceptable.
  • 3
    School shoes and socks as prescribed in the uniform policy must be worn at all times. Sports shoes are allowed only during physical education classes.
  • 4
    Students must maintain clean and neat appearance — hair must be properly groomed. Extreme hairstyles or coloring is not permitted.
  • 5
    Wearing of excessive jewellery or accessories is not allowed. Simple, minimal accessories are acceptable.
📚

Academic Rules

Classroom conduct and learning standards

  • 1
    Students must bring all required books, stationery and materials to class each day as per the timetable.
  • 2
    All homework and assignments must be completed and submitted on the due date. Repeated failure to submit homework will be reported to parents.
  • 3
    Active participation in classroom activities, discussions and group work is expected from all students.
  • 4
    Students must maintain neat and organized notebooks. Notebooks are subject to periodic review by teachers and parents.
  • 5
    Cheating, plagiarism or academic dishonesty of any kind is a serious offense and will result in disciplinary action including possible suspension.
  • 6
    Students are expected to respect and listen to teachers during class. Disrupting lessons will not be tolerated.
📝

Examination Rules

Conduct during tests and examinations

  • 1
    Students must report to the examination hall at least 10 minutes before the examination begins.
  • 2
    Bringing any unfair means into the examination hall — notes, electronic devices, or any form of communication — is strictly prohibited.
  • 3
    Students must follow the seating arrangement assigned by the school. Changing seats without permission is not allowed.
  • 4
    No communication between students is permitted during examinations. Talking, signaling or any form of exchange will be treated as malpractice.
  • 5
    Students who are absent for an examination must provide a valid medical certificate to be considered for a supplementary examination.
⚖️

Discipline & Consequences

Disciplinary process and consequences

  • 1
    The school follows a graduated disciplinary process: verbal warning → written warning → parent notification → suspension → expulsion, depending on the severity of the offense.
  • 2
    Serious offenses such as violence, theft, substance abuse, or vandalism may result in immediate suspension or expulsion without prior warning.
  • 3
    Parents will be notified promptly of any serious disciplinary issue. Cooperation between school and parents is essential for student welfare.
  • 4
    Suspended students will not be permitted on school premises during the period of suspension and must make up missed academic work independently.
👨‍👩‍👧

Parent & Guardian Guidelines

Responsibilities of parents and guardians

  • 1
    Parents must attend Parent-Teacher Meetings (PTM) when scheduled. Regular communication between school and home is critical for student success.
  • 2
    Any change in contact details — address, phone number, or emergency contacts — must be communicated to the school office promptly.
  • 3
    Parents should not visit classrooms without prior appointment. All visitors must report to the school office first.
  • 4
    Parents are expected to support and reinforce school rules and values at home to ensure consistent student development.
  • 5
    School fees must be paid on time as per the fee schedule. Late payment may result in student being unable to sit for examinations.
🚌

Transport Rules

Guidelines for school bus use

  • 1
    Students must be ready at their designated stop before the scheduled bus arrival time. The bus will not wait for late students.
  • 2
    Proper conduct must be maintained on the school bus at all times. Students must remain seated and avoid disturbing the driver.
  • 3
    Students must take care of their belongings on the bus. The school is not responsible for items lost on the school bus.
  • 4
    Any misbehavior on the bus will be reported to the school and may result in the cancellation of transport facilities for that student.
🏠

Hostel Rules

Guidelines for boarding students

  • 1
    Hostel students must follow the daily hostel schedule — including wake-up time, meal times, study hours, and lights-out time.
  • 2
    Leaving the hostel premises without permission from the hostel warden is strictly prohibited.
  • 3
    Students must maintain cleanliness in their rooms and shared areas. Weekly room inspections will be conducted.
  • 4
    Visitors are only allowed during designated visiting hours with prior approval from the hostel warden. Parents may visit on weekends.
  • 5
    Use of personal electrical appliances (heaters, irons etc.) is not permitted in hostel rooms without express permission.

📌 A Note from the School Administration

These rules are not meant to restrict students — they are designed to create a fair, safe and productive environment where every student at New Model can reach their full potential. We appreciate the cooperation of students and parents in upholding these standards. For any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact the school administration.
