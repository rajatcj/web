Our Facilities

New Model provides a rich, modern campus environment — every resource designed to support outstanding learning and student wellbeing.

A Campus Built for Excellence

Located in the serene surroundings of Nagdhunga, Pokhara, our campus offers a peaceful and stimulating environment for learning. Every facility has been carefully developed to meet the growing needs of students and to match international standards of education.

From well-stocked libraries and modern labs to spacious sports grounds and hygienic cafeterias — New Model ensures students have everything they need to thrive.

Infrastructure

All Facilities in Detail

📚

Library

A comprehensive, spacious library stocked with textbooks, reference books, periodicals, and educational materials to inspire curiosity and support deep, independent learning.

  • Wide collection of academic and reference books
  • Quiet, well-lit reading spaces
  • Periodicals and educational journals
  • Available for students during all school hours
🚌

Transportation

An extensive transportation system covering all major areas of the Pokhara valley, ensuring students have safe, reliable, and timely transport to and from school every day.

  • Multiple bus routes covering Pokhara valley
  • Experienced and responsible drivers
  • Regular vehicle maintenance for safety
  • Punctual pickup and drop schedules
🏟️

Sports & Recreation Centers

A wide-ranging, unique sports and recreation complex designed to foster teamwork, fitness, discipline, and the spirit of healthy competition among all students.

  • Outdoor sports grounds for cricket, football, athletics
  • Indoor games and recreational areas
  • Annual Sports Meet organized each year
  • Trained sports instructors and coaches

Hi-Tech Computer Laboratories

State-of-the-art computer laboratories with high-speed internet access and regularly updated hardware and software, preparing students for a technology-driven world.

  • Latest desktop computers with updated software
  • High-speed broadband internet connectivity
  • Qualified ICT teachers and lab assistants
  • Practical sessions for all grade levels

Ultra-Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms are designed for maximum learning effectiveness — spacious, well-ventilated, well-lit, and equipped with modern teaching aids and interactive learning tools.

  • Spacious, well-ventilated classroom spaces
  • Adequate natural lighting and seating
  • Teaching aids and visual learning tools
  • Separate rooms for each grade level

Cafeteria

Affordable, hot, hygienic, and nutritious meals are served daily in our on-campus cafeteria, ensuring students are fueled and energized throughout the school day.

  • Healthy and nutritious daily meal options
  • Maintained to high hygiene standards
  • Affordable pricing for all students
  • Clean, comfortable dining space
🏠

Hostel

Spacious, secure and well-managed hostel facilities for outstation students, providing a comfortable home-away-from-home with a proper environment for study and rest.

  • Spacious, well-furnished rooms
  • Secure and supervised premises
  • Quiet study environment for boarders
  • Nutritious meals included

Student Council

Our active Student Council provides students with opportunities for leadership, decision-making, and personal development — empowering them to be responsible citizens.

  • Elected student body representing all grades

  • Develops leadership and civic responsibility

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our facilities? Find answers to the most common queries below.

What transport facilities are available for students?

+
We operate an extensive bus network covering all major areas of the Pokhara valley. Students can avail the transport service at an affordable monthly fee. Routes and schedules are available from the school office.

Is the hostel facility available for girls too?

+
Yes, the hostel facility is available for both male and female students with separate, supervised accommodations. The hostel environment is safe, secure, and conducive to study.

How is the library managed for student access?

+
The library is open during all school hours. Students can borrow books using their school library card. A librarian is always available to assist students in finding resources.

Are sports facilities available for all students?

+
Yes, sports and recreational facilities are available to all enrolled students. We encourage active participation in sports as part of holistic development. Annual Sports Meet is held each year.

Are computers available for all grade levels?

+
Our computer laboratory serves students from all grade levels. Scheduled practical classes ensure every student gets hands-on experience with modern computers and internet resources.

What food is available in the cafeteria?

+
The cafeteria offers a variety of healthy, hot and hygienic meals at affordable prices. The menu is rotated regularly to provide nutritional balance for growing students.
