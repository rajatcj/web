Who We Are

Cera Dental Clinic
— A Place of Trust

Cera Dental Clinic was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a smile they're proud of. Located on Nayabazar Road in the heart of Pokhara, we've created a space that combines clinical excellence with genuine warmth.

Our clinic is known for its exceptional cleanliness, natural light-filled spaces, and cutting-edge dental equipment. From routine check-ups to complex treatments, we approach each case with the same level of care and precision.

Led by Dr. Shisham and a passionate team of dental professionals, Cera Dental has quickly become one of Pokhara's most trusted and beloved dental destinations — reflected in our perfect 5-star Google rating.
