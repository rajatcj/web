Our Story

Built for Pokhara, Built for You

Midtown Dental was founded with a clear vision: to create a dental clinic in Pokhara that combines clinical excellence with a genuinely relaxed, friendly atmosphere. We noticed that most people avoided dentists not because of the procedures — but because of the experience.

So we fixed that. Appointment-based scheduling eliminates waiting crowds. A spacious, modern lounge replaces the typical cramped waiting area. Friendly, trained staff replace the cold, clinical experience people dread. And advanced, fully sterilized equipment ensures every treatment is safe, precise and effective.
