Surgical Specialist

Dr. Dhruba Chandra Poudel

BDS, MDS · Consultant Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon

Dr. Dhruba Chandra Poudel is our consultant oral and maxillofacial surgeon, holding both BDS and MDS qualifications. As an NMC-registered specialist (NMC No. 8350), he brings an exceptional level of surgical expertise to our team.

His area of expertise spans complex extractions, jaw surgery, facial trauma management, cyst removal, and advanced implant surgeries. Dr. Poudel is known for his precision, calm demeanor, and thorough patient consultations.
