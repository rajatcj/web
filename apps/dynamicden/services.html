Preventive Care

Prevention is Better
Than Cure

🦷

Dental Check-Up & Cleaning

Comprehensive oral examination, professional cleaning, and early detection of dental issues before they become serious problems.

  • Full oral health assessment
  • Plaque & tartar removal
  • Gum health evaluation
  • X-ray diagnostics when needed
🔬

Fluoride Treatment

Professional fluoride application to strengthen enamel and protect teeth against decay, especially important for children and high-risk adults.

  • Enamel strengthening
  • Cavity prevention
  • Safe for all ages
🛡️

Dental Sealants

Protective coating applied to the chewing surfaces of back teeth to shield against bacteria and prevent decay in hard-to-clean grooves.

  • Painless procedure
  • Long-lasting protection
  • Ideal for children & teens

  • Long-lasting results

Dental Veneers

  • Stain-resistant porcelain
  • Covers chips & gaps
  • Natural appearance
  • Durable & long-lasting

Smile Makeover

  • Personalized treatment plan

Restorative Dentistry

Restoring Function
& Beauty

🔧

Dental Fillings

Composite (tooth-colored) and amalgam fillings to restore cavities, matching the natural appearance of your teeth.

  • Tooth-colored options
  • Durable materials
  • Same-day treatment
👑

Dental Crowns & Bridges

Porcelain and zirconia crowns to cap damaged teeth, and bridges to replace missing teeth with a fixed solution.

  • Zirconia & porcelain options
  • Natural look & feel
  • Multi-year durability
🌿

Root Canal Treatment

Advanced endodontic treatment to save infected or severely damaged teeth, eliminating pain and preventing extraction.

  • Pain-free procedure
  • Saves natural teeth
  • High success rate
🦷

Dentures & Partials

Custom-fitted removable dentures for full or partial tooth replacement, restoring your ability to eat, speak, and smile comfortably.

  • Full & partial dentures
  • Custom fit
  • Comfortable & natural-looking
🏗️

Dental Implants

Permanent titanium root replacements topped with natural-looking crowns — the gold standard for replacing missing teeth.

  • Permanent solution
  • Preserves jawbone
  • Looks & functions like real teeth
🦷

Inlays & Onlays

Conservative indirect restorations used when a filling is insufficient but a full crown is not yet necessary.

  • Preserves tooth structure
  • Porcelain & composite options
  • Precise & durable
