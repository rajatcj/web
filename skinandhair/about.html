Our Story

Where Expertise Meets Empathy

Pokhara Skin & Hair Clinic (Gandaki Health Care Pvt. Ltd.) was founded with a clear purpose: to bring world-class dermatology care to Pokhara and the Gandaki region.

Our clinic is led by specialist dermatologists who visit regularly from Kathmandu, ensuring patients in Pokhara have access to the same quality of care available in the capital — without the long journey.

Expert Dermatologists

Dr. Prakash Acharya and Dr. Bishnu Prakash Adhikari are renowned dermatologists with specialist training in skin and hair disorders.

Registered Medical Institution

Operating under Gandaki Health Care Pvt. Ltd. — a fully registered and compliant private healthcare entity in Nepal.

Community-Focused Care

Serving patients from Pokhara and surrounding districts — from Nayabazar to Parbat and beyond.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide accessible, expert-level dermatological care to the people of Pokhara and the Gandaki region. We are committed to treating every patient with dignity, using evidence-based practices and the latest medical technologies to deliver outstanding skin and hair health outcomes.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and recognized dermatology institution in western Nepal — a center of excellence where patients receive the highest standard of care, where innovation thrives, and where every person leaves with renewed confidence in their skin and hair health.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, compassion, and continuous learning guide everything we do. We believe that skin health is fundamental to overall wellbeing, and we approach every patient with the care and expertise they deserve.

Our Promise

Every appointment, every consultation, every treatment — delivered with the utmost professionalism and personalized attention. We don't just treat conditions; we build lasting relationships and help patients understand their skin.
