Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Midtown Dental — our services, appointments, pricing and more.

Got Questions?

We Have Answers

What is the best dental clinic in Pokhara?

+
Midtown Dental Clinic & Orthodontic Center at Prithvi Chowk is rated 4.9/5 on Google with 66+ verified reviews, making it one of Pokhara's top-rated dental clinics. We offer comprehensive dental services including orthodontics, fillings, scaling, and oral surgery in a clean, appointment-based environment.

How do I book an appointment?

+
You can book an appointment by calling us at 061-585333 (Tue–Sun, from 9:30 AM) or by using the online booking form on our Book Appointment page. We are fully appointment-based so there are no walk-in queues — your slot is reserved just for you.

Is Midtown Dental appointment-only? Can I walk in?

+
Yes, we are appointment-based only. This is intentional — it means zero waiting, zero crowded waiting rooms, and full attention during your visit. Please call 061-585333 or book online to schedule your slot.

Do you offer orthodontics and braces in Pokhara?

+
Absolutely. Orthodontics is one of our core specialties. We offer traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, and alignment correction treatments for patients of all ages — from children to adults. Book a free orthodontic consultation to get started.

Is the treatment painless? I'm nervous about dentists.

+
We hear this a lot and it's exactly why we've worked hard to make Midtown a truly comfortable, anxiety-free space. We use modern anaesthesia techniques and take our time to ensure every procedure is as gentle and painless as possible. Many of our most anxious patients leave saying it was "nothing like they expected!"

Are your tools sterilized?

+
Yes, 100%. Every instrument used at Midtown Dental is fully sterilized between each patient. Hygiene and infection control are among our highest priorities. You can visit us with complete confidence in the cleanliness and safety of our clinic.

What dental services do you offer?

+
We offer a full range of dental services including: Orthodontics & Braces, Cavity Fillings (composite), Scaling & Polishing, Oral Surgery & Extractions, Teeth Whitening, and Dental Checkups with X-rays. Visit our Services page for full details.

How much do dental services cost?

+
Costs vary depending on the type of treatment. We believe in transparent, honest pricing with no hidden fees. Please contact us at 061-585333 for a cost estimate for your specific treatment, or book a consultation where we can assess your needs and provide a clear price breakdown.

Do you treat children?

+
Absolutely! We welcome patients of all ages including young children. Our gentle, friendly approach is designed to make dental visits comfortable for kids. Starting dental visits early builds great lifelong habits — we recommend bringing children for their first visit by age 6 or 7.

Where are you located?

+
Midtown Dental is located at Prithvi Chowk, Pokhara, Nepal. We are open Tuesday to Sunday from 9:30 AM. Call 061-585333 for directions or find us on Google Maps by searching "Midtown Dental Pokhara."

What days are you open?

+
We are open Tuesday through Sunday, starting from 9:30 AM. We are closed on Mondays. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

How long does a typical appointment take?

+
It depends on the treatment. A routine checkup typically takes 30–45 minutes. Scaling and polishing is usually 45–60 minutes. Fillings vary from 30–60 minutes. Orthodontic consultations are about 45 minutes. We are efficient — one patient noted we saw a family of 5 in just 45 minutes!

Do I need to bring anything to my first visit?

+
Just yourself! For your first visit, it's helpful to bring any previous dental records or X-rays if you have them, and a list of any medications you're currently taking. Arrive a few minutes early to fill in a short patient form.
