Who We Are

Skincare as an
Act of Love

Dear Skin is Pokhara's premier women-owned cosmetic clinic, founded with a simple but powerful belief: your skin deserves the very best care. Situated at the heart of Lakeside, we bring world-class skincare technology to the beautiful hills of Nepal.

Our founder, Aruna Gurung, built Dear Skin with a vision of combining cutting-edge treatments with a calm, nurturing environment — the kind of place where you feel genuinely cared for from the moment you step in.

From international travellers to loyal local clients, we've earned a perfect 5.0 Google rating with over 321 reviews because we never compromise on quality, hygiene, or results.
